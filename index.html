Austin's Weather Challenges

Austin's unique climate presents specific challenges for homeowners. From intense hailstorms in spring to falling branches from our beloved oak trees, Central Texas roofs take a beating like nowhere else.

Hail Alley Impact

Austin sits at the southern edge of "Hail Alley" — making us prone to severe spring storms with damaging hail. These storms can create subtle damage that worsens over time if not detected early.

Heritage Tree Hazards

Our cherished live oaks and pecan trees provide shade but can damage roofs during storms. Falling branches can puncture shingles, creating entry points for water that lead to costly repairs if not addressed quickly.

After every major storm, we see a 300% increase in calls from homeowners who've been approached by roofing companies claiming "total roof replacement" is needed when often just spot repairs will do.
