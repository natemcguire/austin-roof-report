Central Texas Hailstorms

Austin experiences some of the most damaging hailstorms in Texas. In 2021 alone, Travis County saw over $190 million in hail damage claims. The average hailstorm in our area produces stones between 1-2", more than enough to damage most roofing materials.

The problem? Hail damage isn't always immediately visible from the ground. Our drones can detect early signs of impact damage before they lead to leaks and interior damage.

Tree Canopy Concerns

Austin's urban forest is one of our greatest assets‚Äîand one of the biggest threats to our roofs. The average live oak branch that falls during a storm weighs enough to puncture standard shingles, and the city's beloved pecan trees regularly shed limbs during wind events.

Our drone footage can identify branch scrapes, impact points, and pending issues from overhanging limbs before they cause structural damage.
